 Do’s
1.Think before posting – Make sure your content is respectful and appropriate.
2.Protect your privacy – Adjust privacy settings and avoid sharing personal details.
3.Verify information – Check facts before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation.
4.Maintain professionalism – Keep your profile clean and suitable for future employers.
5.Be respectful – Engage politely and avoid arguments or offensive language.
 Don’ts
1.Don’t share sensitive information – Avoid posting your address, phone number, or passwords.
2.Don’t spread fake news – Never forward unverified or misleading content.
3.Don’t engage in cyberbullying – Avoid negative comments, trolling, or harassment.
4.Don’t overshare – Posting too much can affect your privacy and image.
5.Don’t post inappropriate content – Avoid content that can harm your reputation or others.
